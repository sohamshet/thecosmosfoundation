Our Satelites

We are currently contibuting to these satelites. You can launch your own rocket, let me tell you how. When you scroll down to Creative Rockets(also called Donation Rockets). Creative Rockets are nothing but Creative Topics to which you can contribute to; example: If there are rockets initially for Video creators and Artists who paint. You can contribute to it. We will be slowly expanding to various Creative fields STEP-BY-STEP. Once you find a rocket that pursuits any Creative Zone in which you can unleash your skills. Then the final step launching your creative rocket. You need to head towards the launchpad (the creative supplements form), fill in the details attach the digital files of your work whether its a picture of your Drawing or a file of your blog story. Make sure you submit one Creative Supplement at a time if you have multiple rockets to launch. Then it's our job to pitch your rockets to different Satelites the Space Stations(Organisation to which we pitch your work for example: The Times of India or Scholastic Publications, etc.) These space stations will license your work and credit you. We will send you mail once your rocket has been pitched to a Space Station. The money we raise for your work buy pitching your Rocket to one of the Space Stations is used as fuel (Donation Money) for our Satelites as listed below and a fractional percentage for gross expenses of the organisation. After this, Congratulations! You have successfully become a Donation Pilot(Creative Donor).
